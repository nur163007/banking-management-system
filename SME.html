Home     SME    SME Products


BMS SME Banking offers comprehensive financial solutions for the CMSMEs in Bangladesh. In its product lines, there are innovative and customized products for different sectors of SMEs, there are flexible Funded and Non Funded Facilities. Different customized products are introduced to cater specific industry i.e. Light Engineering and Krishijat (Agro Processing) or specific segment i.e. for Women Entrepreneur to fulfill their unique needs. Deposit products were also customized keeping the nature and needs of CMSME in mind.
